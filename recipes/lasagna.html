Indulge in this satisfying lasagna, baked to golden perfection with a bubbly, cheesy top.

A hearty portion of homemade lasagna is pictured, highlighting the generous layers of savory meat sauce, likely made with tomatoes and ground meat, interspersed with creamy ricotta and topped with melted mozzarella. The distinctive ruffled edges of the lasagna noodles are clearly visible.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried basil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps:

  1. Prepare the meat sauce: In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium-high heat. Drain off any excess fat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Prepare the Ricotta Mixture: In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese. Mix well. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the Noodles: In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese. Mix well. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Preheat Oven: In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium-high heat. Drain off any excess fat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Assemble the Lasagna: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Bake: Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted, bubbly, and lightly browned.
  7. Rest: Let the lasagna stand for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. This helps it hold its shape. Garnish with fresh parsley, if desired.
